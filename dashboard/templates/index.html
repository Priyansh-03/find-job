Reads input/user.csv. Check at least one: all job boards (Greenhouse, Ashby, Workday, JobSpy for Indeed/Google, …) or risk ip (JobSpy only: LinkedIn when checked, else Indeed). All job boards without risk ip does not run JobSpy for LinkedIn—check risk ip to add it (alone or together with all boards). Risk ip does not crawl Notion’s board directly—it only queries LinkedIn; a row can still link to Ashby/Notion if that is where the employer listed the job on LinkedIn. Experience columns in CSV are not used by the fetcher.
